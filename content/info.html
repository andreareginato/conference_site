Where is the conference?



How to reach the conference.

  • By public transport:
    • Yellow underground to Zara and then Tram 7 to "Università Bicocca Scienza" (11 stops)
    • Red underground to Precotto and then Tram 7 to "Università Bicocca Scienza" (4 stops)
  • By train:
    • Stazione Centrale: take the Bus 87 to "Teatro Arcimboldi" (11 stops)
    • Stazione Garibaldi: take the suburban train to "Milano Greco Pirelli" (1 stop)
  • By airplane:
    • Linate: take the Bus 73 to "San Babila" (16 stops), then the red underground to "Precotto" (9 stops) and finally the Tram 7 to "Università Bicocca Scienza" (4 stops)"
